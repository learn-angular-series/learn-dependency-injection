## learn-dependency-injection

此项目专门用来解释 Angular 的依赖注入特性，分支如下：

1. master
1. host-decorator
1. injectable-decorator
1. injector-decorator
1. optional-decorator
1. self-decorator
1. singleton
1. skipsel-decorator
